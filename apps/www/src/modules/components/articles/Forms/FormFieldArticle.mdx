## Synopsis

### `FormField`s

The `FormField` abstraction envelopes _at least_ the `FormControl` and `Label` components. It may also slot `HelpText` and `FormFeedback` (if provided) into the `FormField` template.

In the example below, we look at a basic usage of the `FormField` component.

<FormFieldExample />

Forms are composed of one or many `FormField` (in our contrived example, there's only one). `FormFields`

```jsx
<FormField
  value={this.state.values.foo}
  error={this.state.errors.foo}
  touched={this.state.touched.foo}
  onBlur={this.handleBlur}
  onChange={this.handleChange}
  Component={Input}
  label="Foo"
  helpText={this.state.helpText}
  ref={el => (this.myRef = el)}
  name="foo"
  autoComplete="off"
  placeholder="Placeholder..."
/>
```
